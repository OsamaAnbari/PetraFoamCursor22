Who We Are

Petra Foam is Jordan's premier manufacturer of industrial insulation solutions. With over a decade of experience, we've established ourselves as a trusted partner in providing high-quality insulation materials for both residential and commercial applications.

Our commitment to innovation and sustainability drives us to deliver products that meet the highest international standards while supporting Jordan's growing construction and industrial sectors.
