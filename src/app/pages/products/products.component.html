XPS Insulation

Extruded Polystyrene (XPS) foam boards provide excellent thermal insulation and moisture resistance. Ideal for roofing, walls, and foundation applications.

Technical Specifications

Density 32-35 kg/m³
Thermal Conductivity 0.028-0.031 W/mK
Compressive Strength 300-700 kPa
Water Absorption < 0.7% by volume

EPS Insulation

Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) foam provides cost-effective thermal insulation solutions. Perfect for both residential and commercial construction projects.

Technical Specifications

Density 15-20 kg/m³
Thermal Conductivity 0.035-0.040 W/mK
Compressive Strength 100-200 kPa
Water Absorption < 3% by volume
